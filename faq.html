Frequently Asked Questions


Which destinations does this website cover?

It can help you plan what to see and do in every country in the world. With information on 47,000 destinations across 226 countries at your fingertips, you can find great things to do as easily in smaller locales like Ravenna, Italy as in world-famous cities like Rome. And if you're not sure exactly where you'd like to travel to, our planner can determine which places—both off-the-beaten-path towns and world-famous cities—best suit you.

I'm not sure where to go. Can Travel Freak help?

Sure! Based on the types of attractions you like and the general ages of the people traveling with you, It can automatically select destinations.
You can also create multiple plans to compare before settling on one. For example, if you're trying to decide between two different places, you can build plans for each and decide which one you like best. If you need more help, you can use our collaboration features to ask your friends for advice on your plans.

How do I edit the destinations in my plan?

Click on the Route tab on the top navigation bar. Here, you can mouse over each destination to access options for changing your route order, adjusting the time spent in each destination, and deleting destinations.
To add destinations, use the +Add destination button to search for specific towns and cities or browse ones in the countries you're planning to visit.
You can also explore recommended destinations at the bottom of the page.

How do I adjust the duration I spend at an attraction?

n Calendar view on the Day by Day tab, hover your mouse over the bottom border of the attraction's time block and then click and drag the border up or down to adjust the duration of your visit.
Alternatively, you can click on the attraction block and then the Edit prompt to set the arrival and departure time.
If you need help resolving scheduling conflicts, click on the notification icon on the top navigation bar (it turns red when a conflict arises). Then, click the prompt for help fixing the issue and select the option for the software to recalculate the plan for the destination where the conflict occurs.

Why should I create an account?

If you have an account, it automatically saves any plan you create while logged in. You can then access your plans from other computers and mobile devices. When you're signed in, you can also collaborate on your plans with others.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, navigate to your account name in the top right corner, select My Account and click on Delete my account.
